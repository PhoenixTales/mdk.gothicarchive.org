Gothic MDK Documentation

About

The GMDK (GOTHIC Mod Development Kit) was released by Piranha Bytes in 2001, to be used for non-commercial modifications. Now it is updated and curated by Phoenix Tales. See our Changelog for details.

GOTHIC runs on the ZenGin developed by the Mad Scientists from 1998 to 2001. Since its release the Community has developed various additional modding tools and researched and documented the engine extensively. Phoenix Tales has collected these tools and documentations to create an international Gothic modding hub in form of this website.
